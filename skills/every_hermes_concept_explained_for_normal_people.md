# Every Hermes Concept Explained for Normal People

## Introduction

This skill covers the fundamental concepts of Hermes, a powerful AI agent, and how it can be used to improve your life.

## Steps

1. Think of Hermes as your dog, which lives with you and gets smarter over time.
2. Hermes is like your beautiful Labrador, but it's a contractor, not a chatbot.
3. Hermes is an AI with access to tools, which can be your Gmail, calendar, or other things.
4. The more you use Hermes, the better it gets, and the more specific it is with you.
5. Hermes can be accessed from over 22 different interfaces, such as Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp.
6. You can connect and chat to the same AI intelligence from any platform that you like.
7. Hermes is like an operator sitting in the middle, and you can call into it from any interface, and it will route every single one of them to you.
