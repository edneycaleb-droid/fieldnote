# Building a Comprehensive Second Brain with AI
## What is a Second Brain?
A second brain is a system that organizes all your thoughts and ideas, and provides insights and knowledge on autopilot.
## How to Build a Second Brain
1. Create a digital notebook like Obsidian to store your notes and ideas.
2. Use a large language model like ChatGPT, Grok, or Gemini to write and interlink your notes.
3. Set up a system to feed your notes and ideas into the model, and ask it questions or deliver insights to you in a scheduled manner.
4. Organize and connect your notes and data using the LLM Wiki.
5. Utilize the model to generate insights and connections.
