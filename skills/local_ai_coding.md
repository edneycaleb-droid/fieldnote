# Local AI Coding: Is It Worth It?

## Description
This skill teaches the practical aspects of local AI coding, including the importance of speed, quality of output, compatibility, and cost.

## Steps
1. Understand the importance of speed in local AI coding
2. Compare the quality of output from different local models
3. Consider the compatibility of local models with various tools and services
4. Evaluate the cost of local AI coding versus cloud-based alternatives
5. Learn about the benefits of using local models, such as control and the ability to pick the model
